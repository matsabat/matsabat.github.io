Press Kit - Bot Hunter

Bot Hunter is a fast-paced roguelite shooter with modular weapons. Play as a vacuum cleaner bounty hunter.
Use color theory to clean the galaxy of bot scum and collect rewards to pay your bills.

Trailer

Features

Modular Weapon System
craft and swap different modules to adjust weapon
Color Theory Based Combat
well-matched color of your weapon increases your damage, use it to your advantage!
Hacks
make every mission unique by choosing most suitable boosts along the way
Roguelite Experience
you don’t level up, you only gain experience and skill as you learn during the runs
